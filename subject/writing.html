Writing

Don't you know better than to wake a man at three in the afternoon?

I don’t always write, and I’m not even sure I’m even decent: I certainly haven’t had any training. Even so, writing is still fun. From time-to-time, I’ll have inspiration for a haiku1, or a very short story, and eventually I might even think it’s gotten good enough to share.

  1. I write real haiku, as in 俳句, not English-language haiku, which is totally different. Compared to Japanese haiku, English haiku are 2-3 times more massive.↩
